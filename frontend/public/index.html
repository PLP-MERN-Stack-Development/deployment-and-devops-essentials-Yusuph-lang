SDG 1 – No Poverty

End poverty in all its forms everywhere

About This Goal

Sustainable Development Goal 1 (SDG 1) aims to eradicate extreme poverty for all people everywhere by 2030. Poverty is not only about lack of income—it includes hunger, limited access to healthcare, education, and opportunities for a better life.

This project is a MERN-powered web app that helps communities, organizations, and individuals track local initiatives, share progress, and identify areas most in need of intervention.

Key Facts

  • Over 700 million people still live in extreme poverty.
  • Poverty affects children disproportionately — one in five live in extreme poverty.
  • Access to education, clean water, and social protection can lift millions out of poverty.

Our App’s Mission

The SDG 1 Bug Tracker empowers local initiatives by helping developers, NGOs, and social innovators track issues in anti-poverty projects. It supports transparency, accountability, and collaboration toward ending poverty globally.
